Founder Ryujin Real Estate

“ Meet Shin Ryujin, the 22-year-old founder of Ryujin Real Estate, hailing from South Korea. Shin's fascination with construction and architecture began in her early years, shaped by the rich cultural tapestry of her homeland. What sets Shin apart is not just her professional prowess but a deeply personal connection to her work. Her aspiration to build a home with her husband, Shin Jodeo, has been a driving force, infusing her projects with a unique blend of passion and purpose. Jodeo, her partner of four years, brings his construction expertise, creating a dynamic synergy that makes their work not just a profession but a joyful collaboration. Despite her youth, Shin Ryujin has already made waves in the industry, earning acclaim for her innovative designs and commitment to excellence. Looking ahead, Shin envisions contributing ”
“ to the development of sustainable and eco-friendly communities, striving to create spaces that go beyond structures, embodying the essence of a true home. Her story is a testament to resilience, creativity, and the belief that age is no barrier to making a significant impact in the world of real estate. Shin Ryujin stands as an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs, showcasing the transformative power of a shared vision and a passion for building spaces that people cherish. ”
